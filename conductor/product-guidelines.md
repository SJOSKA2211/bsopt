# Product Guidelines: Black-Scholes Optimization & ML Platform (BS-Opt)

## Communication & Documentation Style
The primary tone and style for project documentation and internal communication is **Formal & Academic**. All outputs must be precise, detailed, and structured, reflecting the complex quantitative analysis and engineering specifications required for industrial-grade financial systems.

## Brand Messaging & Voice
The BS-Opt brand is built on **Innovation & Technical Mastery**. Our messaging highlights the platform's cutting-edge MLOps capabilities and autonomous optimization, positioning it as a leader in technical sophistication for the financial industry.

## Visual Identity & Design Principles
BS-Opt adheres to a design philosophy that is simultaneously **Professional & Minimalist**, **Modern & High-Tech**, and **Data-Centric & Informative**:
- **Professional & Minimalist:** Clean typography and a neutral color palette to reduce cognitive load during high-density data analysis.
- **Modern & High-Tech:** A dark mode aesthetic with vibrant accents conveys technical depth and advanced ML capabilities.
- **Data-Centric:** Priority is given to rich visualizations, charts, and grids, ensuring critical trading data is always at the forefront.

## External Representation & Community
We maintain an **Open & Collaborative** stance. BS-Opt actively fosters a strong open-source community by encouraging external contributions and maintaining transparent development logs, leveraging global expertise to improve platform resilience.

## Knowledge Management
Clarity and accuracy are maintained through a **Centralized Knowledge Base**. A dedicated documentation site or wiki serves as the single source of truth for high-level concepts, user guides, and troubleshooting, providing a searchable entry point for all stakeholders.
