# Product Guidelines: BS-Opt

## Visual Identity & Aesthetic
*   **Primary Theme:** Professional & Financial Dark Mode. High data density with clean, high-contrast layouts.
*   **Secondary Style:** Modern Minimalist principles applied to high-level summaries to reduce cognitive load during critical events.
*   **Design Philosophy:** "Form follows Function". Grids and industrial-style raw data displays are used where they provide technical clarity, while essential metrics are highlighted using modern UI patterns.

## Communication & Messaging
*   **Tone:** Precise, professional, and technical. Avoid marketing fluff; focus on performance, accuracy, and resilience.
*   **Terminology:** Use standard industry terms for Quant Finance (Greeks, Drift, PSI) and DevOps (Uptime, Latency, Error Rate).

## Dashboard Design Rules
*   **Priority 1: Resilience.** The first dashboard seen should always be "System Health & Error Rates".
*   **Priority 2: Performance.** Real-time latency and throughput for pricing calculations.
*   **Priority 3: Intelligence.** ML model drift (PSI) and RMSE over time.
*   **Visualization:** Use Grafana's industrial-grade components (Time Series, Stat panels, Heatmaps).
